Neighborhood Threads

Fostering a community of women using fashion while reducing the environmental impact of the clothing industry.

About Us

We are a group of high school students that care deeply about protecting our environment. From shein to depop, the rise of fast fashion and unsustainable shopping is quickly becoming a problem not only for the environment, but also for women working tirelessly to produce this high demand of clothes in sweatshops. We want to bring sustainable thrifting back to local communities!
